What is Emotional Intelligence?

It is the ability to recognise and keep track of our peers and our own feelings and emotions, and using that information to progress and improve our thoughts and actions

How is it different to IQ?

IQ is a way to measure our mental abilities and all around intelligence. EQ is more about identifying and managing our emotions and the way we feel.

Why is it important?

EQ can help us change the way we think when emotions run high. If we can notice that we are feeling angry as opposed to letting that emotion run rampant through us, we can identify what is causing us to feel that way and how we can let that negative emotion go.
