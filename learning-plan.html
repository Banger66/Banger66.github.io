I've been a student at the school of hard knocks pretty much my whole life, so trying and failing is a great way for me to learn. However, I'm not the greatest when someone walks me through all the steps on how to get the job done. I can't really get things stuck in my head unless I'm actively doing it, getting stuck and then asking question to get myself un-stuck
